Destinations

Star in the Sky

Location: Low Earth Orbit

In this adventure you will explore the areas where man first started to explore space, you will spend 2 nights at the International Space Station with the opportunity to spend time viewing deep space thru the Hubbel 5 Telescope.

From here time will be alloted to venture out into Low Earth orbit and explore the space junk yards where many collectable artifacts from a bygone era can be purchased and sent back to Earth...


Martian Glory

Location: Low Mars Orbit

In this adventure you will explore the areas where man first colonized space, you will spend 2 nights at the International Mars Base Camp, the same place where the pioneers established the Mars colony.

From here time will be alloted to venture out into Low Mars orbit and visit the Jupiter Cruise Refueling Station.


Lunar Rest

Location: Low Moon Orbit

In this adventure you will explore the areas where man first started to explore space, you will spend 2 nights at the Moon Base Facility with the opportunity to spend time exploring the mine camps at the south pole.

From here Lunar walks can be scheduled on a first come, first served basis. Some groups spend time on the dark side of the Moon visiting ancient ruins from unknown civilations.


Olympus, Europa

Location: Jupiter Orbit

This adventure is not recommended for the faint of heart! You will spend two weeks hiking the highest peak of Europa, Mount Olympus. Many opportunities to take in the views and natuural wonders of one of Jupiters Moons.

Or, you may choose to explore the Adamantium mines and take a shot at discovering new mineral deposits...
